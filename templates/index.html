Features

ChronoChat ChronoChat uses immersive roleplay prompting. The AI assumes the first-person voice of a historical figure, staying in character with authentic vocabulary, biases, and worldview. Modern questions are answered through the lens of the chosen persona.
WildMind WildMind prompts GPT to fully inhabit an animal (real or mythical). It emphasises sensory-rich, non-human descriptions and encourages the user to role-play alongside. Human logic is replaced by instinct, sensation, and poetry.
FixIt Frenzy FixIt Frenzy delivers accurate troubleshooting while wrapping instructions in a chosen persona. A flexible prompt ensures both correctness and exaggerated tone (sarcastic, robotic, cheerful, etc.).
GamePlan Live GamePlan Live alternates between two modes: colourful sportscaster commentary and serious tactical analysis. Prompting encourages dynamic switches mid-conversation, creating both energy and depth.
